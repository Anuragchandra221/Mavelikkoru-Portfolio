It's been an year, always happy to see my people... Let's make it more interesting this time. Welcome to the digitilized version of Maveli...

I was a benevolent and generous king. I ruled without discrimination, and my people were honest, healthy, and happy under my rule.I also temporarily possessed the amrita (nectar of eternal life) obtained by the asuras. The amrita allowed his subjects to bring him back to life after his death in one of the wars between suras (devas) and asuras.I was, thus, immune from death. After many wars, I had conquered heaven and earth.
